Message Percentage

Allows you to calculate what percentage your messages make up of the total messages since a specified date (since you started talking on the server).
Change the example values in the boxes to the message count relevant to you.
For the server messages count, make sure that the after date you have selected is the day before the day you sent your first message (you can find out when you sent your first message by when looking at your message count pressing the "Old" filter button).
